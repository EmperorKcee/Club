Our Story

Founded in 2025, FCMS has quickly risen to become one of the most promising football clubs in the region. Our journey began with a simple vision: to nurture local talent and compete at the highest levels of football.

Through dedication, hard work, and a commitment to excellence, we have built a team that embodies the spirit and passion of Zambian football.

Our Mission

To develop world-class football talent, promote sportsmanship, and inspire the next generation of footballers in Zambia and beyond.

Our Values

  • Excellence in performance
  • Teamwork and unity
  • Passion for the game
  • Integrity and fair play
  • Community engagement
